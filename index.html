Our Privacy Commitment.

This document outlines how PrivLedger handles your data. Because we operate on a local-first basis, our policy is simpler than most: we don't take your data.

1. Data Collection & Processing

PrivLedger is architected as a Zero-Knowledge Application. Unlike traditional financial apps that sync your sensitive data to their servers, PrivLedger processes everything on the edge (your device).

"Personal data, financial ledgers, and transaction metadata are never transmitted, seen, or stored by us."

We have no technical means to access your records, and therefore, we cannot sell, share, or lose your data.

2. Terms of Use

A. Software License

PrivLedger grants you a personal, non-transferable license to use this software for personal financial management. You may not reverse engineer or attempt to extract the source code of the app.

B. Responsibility of Data

Because PrivLedger is 100% offline, you are solely responsible for backing up your data. If your device is lost, stolen, or damaged, PrivLedger Finance cannot recover your financial records.

C. Limitation of Liability

PrivLedger is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. We are not liable for any financial losses, mathematical errors, or data corruption that may occur during the use of the application.

Data Erasure

When you delete the app, your data is gone forever. We do not keep shadow copies or cloud mirrors.
